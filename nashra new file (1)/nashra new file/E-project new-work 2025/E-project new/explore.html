Let’s talk about Istanbul’s nightlife

It’s like this city has a whole different side when the sun goes down. The number of clubs here might make your head spin, and it’s no wonder that the party goes on until sunrise.

Now, let’s break down what you can expect in more detail:

  • Kadikoy: Explore Moda, Kadikoy market zone, Iskele Street, Bahariye Street, Kadife Street, and Caddebostan Bar Street.
  • Uskudar: Enjoy local bars and cafes along the Uskudar coast.
  • Sultanahmet: Enjoy a calm evening with scenic views and visit the laid-back bars around Sultanahmet Square.

Taksim & Istiklal

The heartbeat of Istanbul’s nightlife! Live music, street performers, and rooftop bars everywhere.

  • Istiklal Avenue: Music bars and pubs
  • Rooftop lounges with Bosphorus views
  • Neon-lit nightclubs open till dawn

Bosphorus Nights

Experience Istanbul from a cruise! Enjoy DJ nights, dinners, and dancing on the water.

  • Romantic dinner cruises
  • Party boats with DJs
  • Golden views of the city skyline
